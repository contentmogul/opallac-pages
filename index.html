HOW IT WORKS

Gel-XTND soft gel tips fuse to your natural nail with our 5-in-1 Rubber Base and cure under the Flash Cure LED Lamp. Salon-quality extensions in under 30 minutes. No glue, no acrylics, no damage.

◔
STEP 01

Prep & buff

Clean nails with alcohol wipes. Lightly buff the surface for a clean adhesion. File and shape your natural nail.

◐
STEP 02

Apply tip & base

Sized Gel-XTND tip in place. Brush the 5-in-1 Rubber Base on your nail and the underside of the tip, then press.

◑
STEP 03

Flash cure

Cure each nail under the Flash Cure LED Lamp for 60 seconds. The compact design fits even the longest tips precisely.

◕
STEP 04

Colour & finish

Shape with a file. Apply your favourite Opallac gel colour. Seal with top coat and cure. Wears 3+ weeks.

GEL-XTND VS PRESS-ON

Not all "fake nails" are the same. Soft gel tips fuse to the natural nail. Press-ons sit on top.

Gel-XTND Soft Gel

  • 3 week wear, no lifting
  • Soaks off cleanly with acetone
  • Salon-quality glossy finish
  • Fuses to the natural nail
  • Built for soft gel application

Press-on / Fake Nails

  • Days, not weeks, of wear
  • Glue-on, lifts easily
  • Plastic feel and finish
  • Sits on top of the natural nail
  • Generic application across brands
