HOW IT WORKS

Gel-XTND soft gel tips are made from the same gel polish system you already love. Apply with our 5-in-1 Rubber Base, cure under an LED lamp, and you have a flexible, comfortable extension that moves with your natural nail. No glue. No acrylics. No damage.

◔
STEP 01

Prep your nail

Clean each nail with alcohol wipes. Lightly buff the surface for a flawless bond. File and shape your natural nail to match.

◐
STEP 02

Size your tip

Find the right size from the 14 sizes per box. Brush the 5-in-1 Rubber Base on your natural nail and on the inside of the tip.

◑
STEP 03

Press & cure

Press the tip onto the nail at a slight angle, hold steady, then cure under the LED lamp. The Flash Cure handles each nail precisely.

◕
STEP 04

Shape & finish

File to your preferred length and shape. Apply your favourite Opallac gel colour. Seal with top coat. Wears 3+ weeks.

GEL-XTND VS PRESS-ON

Not all "fake nails" are the same. Gel-XTND is made from the same flexible soft gel as your favourite gel polish. Press-ons are rigid plastic, glue-only, and sit on top of your nail.

Gel-XTND Soft Gel

  • Flexible soft gel, same system as gel polish
  • Pre-shaped with a natural curve at the apex
  • Applied with gel, cured under an LED lamp
  • Moves with the natural nail, less lifting and breakage
  • Soak-off removal, no ripping or force
  • Infillable with Builder Gel for refresh
  • Thin, lightweight, feels comfortable

Press-on / Fake Nails

  • Rigid plastic, no flexibility
  • One-size shape, sits on top of the nail
  • Glue-only, no curing
  • Lifts and pops off, days not weeks
  • Peeled or ripped off, can damage the nail
  • Single-use, no refresh option
  • Plastic feel and finish
